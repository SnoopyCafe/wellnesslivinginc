Frequently Asked Questions

What services are included in a stay at Wellness Living Inc?

Your stay includes access to quaint, serene rooms, 24/7 licensed medical care, concierge and transportation services, and a full range of amenities designed for relaxation and recovery. Optional add-ons like IV therapy, lymphatic drainage massages, and personalized nutrition plans are available to enhance your healing experience.

How do I book an appointment at your clinic, and what is the process?

Booking an appointment is easy. Schedule your visit through our website, call during business hours, or visit us in person. After booking, you'll receive a confirmation with appointment details. If unsure about a treatment, we recommend starting with a consultation to discuss your concerns.

Do I need to follow any special instructions to prepare for my treatment?

Preparation varies by treatment. For most procedures, avoid heavy skincare products, makeup, or sun exposure beforehand. For treatments like laser therapy or chemical peels, you may need to stop using products like retinoids a few days prior. Tailored instructions will be provided when you book your appointment.

Are the treatments safe for people with sensitive or reactive skin types?

Yes, our treatments are designed to be safe and effective for all skin types, including sensitive or reactive skin. Our dermatologists take the time to understand your skin's unique needs and will adjust the treatment to ensure safety and optimal results. If you have specific allergies or sensitivities, please let us know during consultation.

How long does it usually take to see noticeable results?

The timeframe for visible results varies by treatment and skin condition. Chemical peels and facials often show improvements immediately, while laser therapy or acne treatments may take multiple sessions. During your consultation, we'll provide a clear timeline for your results.

IV Vitamin Therapy

What are benefits of IV Vitamin Therapy?

IV Vitamin Therapy offers numerous benefits, including enhanced energy levels, improved immune function, rapid rehydration, detoxification, and accelerated healing, especially beneficial for post-surgical recovery.

How does IV Vitamin Therapy aid recovery after plastic surgery?

After plastic surgery, IV Vitamin Therapy can significantly aid recovery by delivering essential nutrients directly to your bloodstream, which helps reduce inflammation, minimize bruising and swelling, boost your body's natural healing processes, and replenish energy.

Is IV Vitamin Therapy right for you?

IV Vitamin Therapy is suitable for many individuals seeking to optimize their health, enhance recovery, or boost their immune system. We recommend a consultation with our specialists to assess your specific needs and determine the most beneficial therapy for you.

What You Need to Know About IV Vitamin Therapy

IV Vitamin Therapy involves administering vitamins and minerals directly into your bloodstream, bypassing the digestive system for maximum absorption. Sessions are typically comfortable and relaxing, lasting between 30 to 60 minutes. Our team will guide you through the process and answer any questions you may have.
